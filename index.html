Introduction

Overview of the Hangman Game Project

The Hangman Game is a popular word guessing game where the player tries to reveal a hidden word one letter at a time.


In this project, the hidden word is a randomly selected month name. The player has limited lives and loses one life for every incorrect guess.


The project demonstrates the use of loops, arrays, functions, randomization, and ASCII art in C++ programming.

Working Principle

How the Hangman Game works

Step 1

Select a random month from the predefined list.

Step 2

Hide all characters using X symbols.

Step 3

User guesses letters one by one.

Step 4

Correct guesses reveal matching letters.

Step 5

Incorrect guesses decrease player lives.

Step 6

Game ends when word is guessed or lives become zero.
